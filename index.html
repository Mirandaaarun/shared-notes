Nightlife Version: Shanghai, Chongqing, Chengdu

Shanghai

Best Mix of Bars, Clubs, Rooftops

Clubs: Arkham, Elevator, Dada, TAXX/Found 158.

Bars: Speak Low, Flask, JZ Club.

Rooftops: Char Bar, Flair, Captain Bar.

Best flow: French Concession dinner → Speak Low → Arkham or Elevator.

Chongqing

Best Visual Nightlife City

Clubs: ESMi, SPACE, PLAY HOUSE, Xingxing Dibao.

Bars: Air raid shelter bars, True Color, Code Bar.

Rooftops: Bar 62, M1NT.

Best flow: Hongya Cave view → river cruise → 9th Street club → hotpot after midnight.

Chengdu

Best Soft Night Instead of Hard Clubbing

Vibe: More teahouse, alley, craft drink, and relaxed local bar culture than Shanghai- or Chongqing-style clubbing.

Best zones from your notes: People’s Park area, Kuanzhai Alley, Jinli, Taikoo Li surroundings.

Best flow: Hotpot dinner → teahouse or relaxed cocktail stop → early night before the Xi'an move.

Special recommendation version: if the group cares most about nightlife, Shanghai is the strongest all-around city, Chongqing is the most memorable visual party city, and Chengdu is best treated as a softer recovery night rather than a full club night.

City Nightlife Picks

Shanghai

Best for first big night out. Your notes strongly support the French Concession + Found 158 + hidden cocktail bar pattern. Best nightlife density, easiest English support, and the safest opening city for a mixed group.

Chongqing

Best for the one truly wild night. Use Jiefangbei and Hongya Cave for visuals, then move to 9th Street for the actual club scene. Skip Nanbin Road bars except for a quick scenic drink.

Chengdu

Not the city to force a hard-clubbing night. The stronger play is panda morning, People’s Park afternoon, then a slower dinner-and-drinks night. It works as a recovery stop between Chongqing and Xi'an.

Xi'an and Beijing

Xi'an is better for food-night energy than club energy. Beijing is only the final stop here, so keep it practical unless your departure is late enough to justify a final-night Sanlitun or Ghost Street outing.
