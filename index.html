Bottom Line

These four sightseeing cities are worth it if Beijing is only your exit city, not a full sightseeing stop. The part that is not worth forcing into this trip is extra Beijing touring. With only 9 nights after a long-haul arrival, Shanghai, Chongqing, Chengdu, and Xi'an are the stronger use of time.

Shanghai to Chongqing: Train vs Flight

Option Approximate Time Recommendation
High-speed rail Fastest about 8h 59m; many direct services are 9 to 13+ hours depending on train. Not recommended for this trip. It burns too much daylight.
Direct flight About 3h 10m from Shanghai Hongqiao or about 3h 25m from Shanghai Pudong. Recommended. Even with airport time, it still saves most of the day.
For this itinerary, fly Shanghai → Chongqing, then use high-speed rail for Chongqing → Chengdu, Chengdu → Xi'an, and Xi'an → Beijing.

If You Want Less Rushing

  • Keep Shanghai, Chengdu, and Xi'an as the three real sightseeing bases.
  • Make Chongqing only a same-day stop if the Liziba train view is your main goal.
  • Do not add Beijing sightseeing unless you extend the trip by at least 2 more nights.
